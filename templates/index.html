The Heights

Sunset Heights sits at the heart of Houston's most walkable urban neighborhood. Step outside the gate and you're a short stroll from 19th and 20th Street's independent restaurants, boutiques, and coffee shops — plus the Heights Farmers Market on Saturdays.

The M-K-T trail system connects through White Oak Bayou greenspace directly from the neighborhood, and Heights Boulevard's landmark tree-lined esplanade is minutes away on foot. Yale Street's Whole Foods, Target, and restaurants handle everyday errands without a highway.

Zoned to A-rated Helms Elementary (walkable). Quick access to I-10 and Loop 610 puts Downtown, the Galleria, and both airports within easy reach.
